Three Phases of Experience — Addendum (Raw Dictation)

2026-02-17 · source transcript for Thinking of Life as a Controlled Hallucination

I think I would probably add to this text that I just finished reading A Brief History of Intelligence. So I have been thinking about the process of thinking quite a lot. Sometimes I think about it in relation to artificial intelligence, and sometimes I think about it in relation to my own family. But overall, I've been thinking about it, and these thoughts strike me when I'm just biking to work.

Part of my process is that I've started using the ChatGPT app mostly to record dictations because I find that it's so much better than Siri that I can actually stop, record a dictation, and know that it's going to actually be like 95% accurate instead of total trash like it is with Siri.

As these strike me as I bike through the woods on my way to work, I record them, and they really become articles sometimes. And this one—again—the main part I want to get across is that I'm not woo-woo. I'm not getting high. I'm not doing drugs. I just think it's interesting to think about life as a controlled hallucination. It's a whole different way to frame our world.
